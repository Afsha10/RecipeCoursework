Healthy Ramen

Ramen is a Japnaese noodle dish. I love making this healthy ramen. It is easy to prepare and also super delicous. You should try it too. 😄

Ingredients

For Noodles

  • Brown rice ramen noodles (I do 4 cups of water for 2 noodle cakes)
  • 2 cups of frozen or fresh veggies
  • Prawns
  • Optional: fresh parsley to garnish

For Seasoning

  • 2 tsp veggie stock
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp Chinese 5 spice powder
  • ¼ tsp cumin
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp tamari sauce

Instructions

  1. Bring water to boil, add spices, once boiling, add ramen cakes.
  2. Once noodles are 90% cooked, add in frozen veggies & prawns.
  3. Allow to simmer for a few mins until everything is cooked.
  4. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired and enjoy!
